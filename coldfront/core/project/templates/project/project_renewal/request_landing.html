Note: Currently, only renewals of Faculty Compute Allowances (FCAs) are supported in this form. Renewals for Instructional Compute Allowances (ICAs) and Partner Compute Allowances (PCAs) are coming soon.

Some compute allowances last for a particular allocation period. When they reach their end dates, they must be renewed. Each allowance is tied to a particular PI, and administrators must review that that PI is still eligible. The following allocation types require renewal:

  • Faculty Compute Allowance (FCA): June 1st to May 31st
  • Instructional Compute Allowance (ICA): Semester Start to End Dates*
  • Partner Compute Allowance (PCA): June 1st to May 31st

This form is intended for Principal Investigators (PIs) or Managers of projects. They may renew a particular PI's allowance for the current allocation period. This may involve keeping the PI on the Project they are currently using, or opting to start or stop pooling with other PIs.

If you are filling out the form on behalf of another PI, that PI will be notified by email that a request is being made by you.

* The PI will be contacted to provide a signed Memorandum of Understanding as part of the approval process.
